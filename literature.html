Literary Arts

I enjoy reading creative works. I prefer fiction over other formats like poetry. My favorite genres are fantasy, science fiction, and humor.

My Tastes in Fiction

In elementary school I enjoyed reading fantasy novels geared towards children (things I could find in the children's section of libraries). My favorite series then were Warrior Cats, Guardians of Ga'Hoole, the 39 Clues, Harry Potter, and Percy Jackson. I generally favored books about anthropomorphic animals or teenagers engaging in conflicts hidden from the mundane world.

When I started middle school, popular books and series from the teen section like The Hunger Games, The Fault in Our Stars, Divergent, and The Mortal Instruments were popular, so I starting reading those books, not wanting to miss out.

Around eighth grade, I started reading standalone books more often, since I ran out of trending book series to read. I receive recommendations from my local public library's website and my school's book club.

My Growing Interest in Comics

Right before the end of the school year in seventh grade, I was introduced to manga, comics originating from Japan. From reading manga, I started developing interest in emulating certain author-artists' art styles. In my sophomore year of high school, I began reading comics on the WEBTOON platform. The comics on WEBTOON are from many different countries and are unlike manga in how they are exclusively digital and in color. I enjoy a variety of genres on WEBTOON, ranging from comedy and slice-of-life to sci-fi and fantasy to horror and thriller.
